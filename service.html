We model the confusion between two types of visualization. We define the confusion factor as follows.
Ba,b = P((a∈F∧b∈S)∨(b∈F∧a∈S)|a∈S∨b∈S)
The a and b are two different visualizations. The S and F represent the set of successful voting and the set of failed voting.
