Methodology

Image Extraction

To ensure the papers are correct and complete, we refer to vispubdata.org, which has provided the title and digital object identifier(DOI) of the IEEE VIS papers.

Taxonomy

We started from the taxonomy by Borkin et al. Then we merge some of the categories because of the similarity of their definition (called synonym set). After that, we have formed a taxonomy with 12 categories and 57 types.

Visualization Type Annotation

In a task, each participant is asked to select all the visualization types occurred in the images. If a image does not contain any types of visualization in our scope, an empty annotation can be submitted. After fine tuning, the average accuracy on the “gold standards” increased to 89.3%, and after majority vote, the accuracy reaches 95%.

Bounding Box Annotation

The crowdsource department (called JD Zhongzhi5) of JD.com, Inc. annotated bounding box. During annotation, the data annotators full-time works for our annotation tasks, and the data reviewers serve for quality control by randomly sampling 15% of the results and double-checking.
